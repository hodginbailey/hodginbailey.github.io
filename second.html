  • Golf Course

    If you're looking to play a few rounds of golf on your vacation to Catalina Island, you're in luck. We offer two options for you, the Catalina Island Golf Course and the Miniature Golf Gardens.

    Miniature Golf:

    Enjoy the day with visiting our unique 18-hole course with all the family fun and competetion you can receive. Hours are Sunday thru Friday - 9 am to 5:30 pm (last tee off 4:30 pm) and Saturday - 9 am to 8:30 pm (last tee off 7:30 pm). Children are $10.00 ages 2-11 and Adults $12.00

    Regular Golf Course:

    This scenic, 2,100-yard course has nine challenging holes, two sets of tees for 18-hole play. The long, narrow course meanders through a canyon setting toward the ocean. The small greens and deep bunkers are enough to challenge any golfer. A driving net is available. Deer, hawks and island fox frequent the area. Open Daily - 7 am to 6 pm 9-Hole - $41 Mon-Thurs, $50 Fri-Sun and 18-Hole - $65 Mon-Thurs, $75 Fri-Sun

  • Boating Tours

    A great way to explore and experience the beauty of Catalina Island is by getting out on the water. The sparkling ocean surrounding the island is filled with dolphins, whales, sea lions, and an array of fish including the California flying fish and the Garibaldi, the bright orange fish that is the official marine fish of California. Includes different activities like fishing, glass bottom boat voyage and more!
